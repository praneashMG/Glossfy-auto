General Detailing

Do I need to provide water or power?

No, you don't need to provide anything! Our mobile detailing vans are fully equipped with our own spot-free deionized water tanks and super-quiet industrial generators. We just need your keys and enough space to open the doors.

How long does a full detail take?

Depending on the package and the condition of your vehicle, a standard Signature Detail takes roughly 2 to 3 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating packages can take anywhere from 6 to 12 hours over one or two days.

Can you remove deep scratches?

We can remove or significantly reduce the appearance of swirl marks, wash marring, and light scratches through our multi-stage paint correction process. As a general rule: if you can catch your fingernail in the scratch, it has penetrated the clear coat and will require a body shop to repaint.

Ceramic Coatings

What exactly is a 9H Ceramic Coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer derived from silica (SiO2). When applied by hand, it chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory clear coat, creating a semi-permanent, glass-like layer of protection. It provides extreme gloss, blocks UV rays, and makes the car highly hydrophobic (water-repellent).

How long does the coating last?

Our professional-grade 9H coatings are rated to last anywhere from 3 to 5 years, depending on the package you choose and how well the vehicle is maintained. Routine maintenance washes using pH-neutral soaps will ensure maximum longevity.

Can I take my coated car through a drive-thru wash?

We strongly advise against it. Automated car washes with spinning brushes use harsh, abrasive chemicals and dirty bristles that will induce swirl marks and degrade the ceramic coating much faster. Stick to touchless washes or, preferably, hand-washing.

Memberships

How does the monthly membership work?

You select a tier (Enthusiast, Perfectionist, or Showroom) based on how often you want us to come out. Once subscribed, you set a recurring schedule. We show up automatically at your chosen location to maintain your vehicle's pristine condition.

Can I pause or cancel my membership?

Absolutely. You have total control through your User Dashboard. You can easily skip a scheduled detail, change your date, or cancel your subscription entirely with no hidden fees or penalties.

Policies & Prep

What should I do before you arrive?

Please remove all personal items, trash, and valuables from the vehicle, including the trunk if you would like it vacuumed. We do not open closed center consoles or gloveboxes unless requested and emptied beforehand.

What happens if it rains on my scheduled day?

Because we are a mobile service, extreme weather (heavy rain, freezing temperatures) can prevent us from working properly. If the weather is uncooperative and you do not have a covered garage we can use, we will contact you to reschedule at your earliest convenience.
