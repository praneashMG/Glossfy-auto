Application Guide

3 Steps to Glass-Like Shine

Using our flagship Graphene Ceramic Spray is incredibly easy. Professional results without the professional price tag.

1

Prep the Surface

Wash the vehicle thoroughly to remove loose dirt. For best results, decontaminate the paint with a clay bar.

2

Spray & Spread

Spray 2-3 mists of coating onto a clean microfiber towel or applicator pad. Wipe evenly across one panel at a time.

3

Buff to Shine

Wait 30-60 seconds until it flashes (looks hazy), then immediately buff off with a dry microfiber towel to reveal the gloss.
